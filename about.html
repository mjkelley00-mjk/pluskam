Our Story

Founded in 2009, +kam has grown from a Chicago-only activation company to a nationwide consumer engagement agency.

While experiential activations across the US and Canada are still a core competency, +kam has diversified its services to include research and content development as the conversation between brands and their customers has evolved.

With the office team in Chicago and an established network of fully-vetted vendors and contractors, +kam has the expertise to engage consumers how and where brands seek to reach them.
